Google + Flickr

In combination with Google Maps and Flickr, this app allows you to search through photos on Flickr that have geotags.
The photos are then marked on Google Maps and allows you to see where in the world a photo was taken.

To begin, type a subject in the search bar below. Some of the best results that utilize the map include 'landscape', 'cityscape' and 'landmarks'.
